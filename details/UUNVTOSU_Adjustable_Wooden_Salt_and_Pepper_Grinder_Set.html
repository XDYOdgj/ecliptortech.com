Premium Wooden Salt and Pepper Grinder Set

Elevate your kitchen with this elegantly designed wooden salt and pepper grinder set. Thoughtfully crafted for both form and function, it adds a touch of sophistication to your dining table while ensuring precision in your cooking.

  • Adjustable grinding settings allow you to choose between coarse and fine textures for the perfect flavor control.
  • Durable ceramic grinding core ensures long-lasting efficiency and resistance to wear.
