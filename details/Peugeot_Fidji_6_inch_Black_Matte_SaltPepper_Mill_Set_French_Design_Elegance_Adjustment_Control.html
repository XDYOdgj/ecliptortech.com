Peugeot Fidji Salt & Pepper Mill Set

Elevate your culinary experience with the Peugeot Fidji Salt & Pepper Mill Set. Combining French design with modern functionality, these elegant mills bring a touch of sophistication to every meal.

Stylish & Durable Design

The Peugeot Fidji set features a sleek black matte finish that complements any kitchen decor. Its robust construction ensures long-lasting use and an effortlessly stylish presence at your dining table.

With an adjustable knob, you have full control over the grind consistency, allowing you to tailor the seasoning to your preferences. Whether you're a seasoned chef or a home cook, this precision ensures every dish is perfectly seasoned.

Made from precision-engineered steel and hand-finished beechwood, these mills are built to deliver professional-grade results with every turn. The two-stage grinding mechanism ensures a fine, even grind for both salt and pepper.

The timeless French aesthetic of the Peugeot Fidji set makes it not only a useful kitchen tool but also a charming addition to your home. Ideal for both everyday cooking and special occasions, this set is a must-have for anyone who values quality and elegance in their cooking.
